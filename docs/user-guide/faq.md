## General

### What is Urban Home School?

Urban Home School is an AI-powered educational platform designed for Kenyan children. It provides CBC-aligned courses across all subjects, combined with a multi-AI tutoring system called The Bird AI that helps students learn at their own pace. The platform supports students from Grade 1 through Grade 12 (or Form 4).

### Who is Urban Home School for?

Urban Home School serves multiple user types:

- **Students**: Grade 1 through 12, the primary learners on the platform.
- **Parents**: Monitor their children's progress, manage payments, and receive AI insights about their child's learning.
- **Instructors**: Create and teach courses, grade assignments, and conduct live sessions.
- **Partners**: Organizations or individuals who sponsor students and fund educational programs.

### Is Urban Home School free?

Urban Home School offers both free and paid content:

- **Free courses**: Available to all registered students at no cost.
- **Paid courses**: Premium courses created by instructors are available for a fee (paid in KES).
- **AI Tutor**: The Bird AI is available to all registered students with their enrolled courses.
- **Registration**: Creating an account is free.

### What devices can I use?

Urban Home School works on any device with a modern web browser:

- **Desktop/Laptop**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
- **Tablet**: iPad, Android tablets
- **Mobile**: Smartphones (Android and iOS)

The platform is responsive and adapts to different screen sizes. For the best experience, we recommend using a device with a screen size of at least 5 inches.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, an active internet connection is required to use Urban Home School. The platform streams course content, AI responses, and other data in real time. Some features (like AI tutoring) require a stable connection.

### Is Urban Home School aligned with the CBC curriculum?

Yes. All courses on Urban Home School are aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). Courses are organized by grade level and learning area according to the CBC framework.

## Accounts

### How do I sign up?

1. Visit the Urban Home School website.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Choose your role (Student, Parent, or Instructor).
4. Fill in your details (name, email, phone number, password).
5. Verify your email address.
6. Log in and complete your profile.

Students under 18 may need a parent to create their account or link their account to a parent account.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your email for a password reset link.
5. Click the link and create a new password.

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. If you still do not receive it, contact support.

### Can I change my role on the platform?

Your role (Student, Parent, Instructor, Partner) is set during registration and determines your dashboard experience. To change your role:

- **Student to Parent**: Not applicable. Create a separate parent account and link your child's student account.
- **User to Instructor**: Apply to become an instructor through the instructor application process. Your application will be reviewed by platform staff.
- **Other changes**: Contact support for assistance.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to **Settings > Account**.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. Confirm the deletion.

Please note that account deletion is permanent. All your data, including course progress, certificates, and conversation history, will be removed. If you are a parent, deleting your account does not delete your child's account.

You can also request account deletion by emailing support@urbanhomeschool.co.ke.

### I am a parent. Can I manage multiple children?

Yes. The Parent Dashboard supports managing multiple children:

1. Link each child's student account to your parent account during their registration or from your dashboard.
2. Your Parent Dashboard shows an overview of all linked children.
3. Click on any child to see their individual progress, grades, and Learning Path.
4. You will receive notifications for all linked children.

## Courses

### How do I enroll in a course?

1. Click **Courses** in the sidebar to browse the course catalog.
2. Use filters to find courses by grade level, subject, or price.
3. Click on a course to view its details.
4. Click **Enroll for Free** (for free courses) or **Enroll** (for paid courses).
5. For paid courses, complete the payment using M-Pesa, PayPal, or a credit/debit card.
6. Once enrolled, the course appears in **My Courses**.

### What is the difference between free and paid courses?

| Aspect | Free Courses | Paid Courses |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Cost | KES 0 | Varies (set by instructor) |
| Content | Basic lessons and quizzes | Full curriculum with advanced content |
| Instructor support | Limited | Direct instructor access, live sessions |
| Certificate | Included | Included |
| AI Tutor | Available | Available |

### How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies. Each course displays an estimated completion time on its detail page. Typical ranges:

- **Short courses**: 2-4 weeks (1-2 hours per week)
- **Standard courses**: 4-8 weeks (2-3 hours per week)
- **Comprehensive courses**: 8-16 weeks (3-5 hours per week)

You can learn at your own pace. There are no strict deadlines for self-paced courses, although some assignments may have due dates set by the instructor.

### Do I get a certificate when I complete a course?

Yes. When you complete all lessons and pass the final assessment with the required minimum score, a digital certificate is automatically generated. See the [Certificates Guide](./uhs-v1/certificates.md) for details.

### A course is not loading. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Refresh the page.
3. Clear your browser cache.
4. Try a different browser.
5. If the problem persists, submit a support ticket with the course name and any error messages.

## AI Tutor (The Bird)

### What is The Bird AI?

The Bird AI is your personal AI tutor on Urban Home School. It uses multiple AI models (Gemini, Claude, Grok, GPT-4) to answer your questions, explain concepts, generate practice quizzes, and help you study across all subjects. See the [AI Tutor Guide](./urban-bird-v1/ai-tutor.md) for details.

### Which AI models does The Bird use?

The Bird uses a multi-AI orchestration system:

| Model | Provider | Primary Use |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Gemini Pro | Google | General education, reasoning, math |
| Claude 3.5 Sonnet | Anthropic | Creative tasks, detailed explanations |
| Grok | X.AI | Research, current events |
| GPT-4 | OpenAI | Fallback for all task types |

The Bird automatically selects the best model for each question. You do not need to choose manually.

### Is The Bird AI safe for children?

Yes. The Bird AI includes multiple safety features:

- **Content filtering**: All responses are filtered for age-appropriate content.
- **Grade-level calibration**: Responses are tailored to the student's registered grade level.
- **Redirect policy**: The Bird will redirect off-topic or inappropriate requests back to educational content.
- **Parental visibility**: Parents can view their child's conversation history.
- **Human oversight**: Platform staff can review flagged conversations.

### Can I customize my AI tutor?

Yes. You can customize several aspects of your AI tutor:

- **Name**: Give your tutor a custom name.
- **Avatar**: Choose from available avatar options.
- **Personality**: Select traits like Friendly, Encouraging, or Serious.
- **Response style**: Choose Concise, Balanced, or Detailed.
- **Subject focus**: Prioritize certain subjects.

Go to **Settings > Agent Profile** or the **Settings tab** in CoPilot to customize.

### Does The Bird AI support voice mode?

Yes. Voice Mode allows The Bird to read responses aloud using AI-generated speech (powered by ElevenLabs). You can:

- Click the speaker icon on any response to hear it read aloud.
- Enable automatic voice mode in Settings.
- Choose from different voice profiles and adjust playback speed.
- Listen in English or Kiswahili.

See the [Voice Mode Guide](./urban-bird-v1/voice-mode.md) for details.

## Payments

### I tried to pay via M-Pesa but the transaction failed. What should I do?

If your M-Pesa payment failed:

1. Check that you entered the correct phone number.
2. Ensure you have sufficient funds in your M-Pesa account.
3. Wait 5 minutes and try again (sometimes there are network delays).
4. Check if you received the STK push on your phone. If not, try initiating the payment again.
5. If the amount was deducted but the course was not unlocked, save your M-Pesa confirmation message and submit a support ticket. Include the confirmation code and amount.

The platform will verify the transaction and resolve the issue, typically within 24 hours.

### What is the refund policy?

- **Within 48 hours**: Full refund available for paid courses if no more than 10% of the content has been accessed.
- **After 48 hours**: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by contacting support.
- **M-Pesa refunds**: Refunds are processed back to the original M-Pesa number within 3-5 business days.
- **PayPal/Card refunds**: Refunds are processed through the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

To request a refund, go to **Wallet > Transaction History**, find the transaction, and click **Request Refund**, or email support.

### How does the wallet work?

Your wallet is a digital balance on the platform:

- **Top up** your wallet using M-Pesa, PayPal, or a credit/debit card.
- **Pay for courses** directly from your wallet balance.
- **Receive refunds** back to your wallet or original payment method.
- **Track transactions** in the wallet transaction history.

### Where can I find my receipts?

1. Go to **Wallet** or **Payments** in the sidebar.
2. Click on **Transaction History**.
3. Each transaction has a receipt available for download.
4. Click the **Download Receipt** button next to any transaction.

### How do instructor payouts work?

Instructors earn revenue from course sales and live session fees:

- **Revenue split**: Instructors receive 70% of course sale revenue; the platform retains 30%.
- **Payout methods**: M-Pesa or bank transfer.
- **Payout schedule**: Instructors can request payouts at any time once earnings exceed a minimum threshold.
- **Processing time**: M-Pesa payouts are processed within 24 hours. Bank transfers take 3-5 business days.

Instructors manage their earnings from the **Earnings** section of their dashboard.

## Technical

### Which browsers are supported?

Urban Home School supports the following browsers (latest two major versions):

| Browser | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| Google Chrome | Yes |
| Mozilla Firefox | Yes |
| Apple Safari | Yes |
| Microsoft Edge | Yes |
| Opera | Yes |
| Internet Explorer | No |

For the best experience, use the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Urban Home School is a web application accessible through any mobile browser. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future development. The web application is fully responsive and provides a near-native experience on mobile devices.

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?

1. Click your profile icon in the top navigation bar.
2. Click **Theme** or look for the theme toggle.
3. Choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** (matches your device setting).

The theme preference is saved and persists across sessions.

### How is my data protected?

Urban Home School takes data protection seriously:

- **Encryption**: All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption.
- **Password security**: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.
- **JWT authentication**: Secure token-based authentication for all API requests.
- **GDPR/DPA compliance**: You can request to download or delete your data at any time.
- **Minimal data collection**: We only collect data necessary for the platform to function.
- **No data sharing**: Your personal data is never shared with third parties without your consent.
- **Regular audits**: The platform undergoes regular security assessments.

For more details, review the Privacy Policy in the Settings page.

### How do I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug or technical issue:

1. Go to **Settings > Help & Support**.
2. Click **Report a Bug**.
3. Describe the issue in detail:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Your browser and device
   - Screenshots if possible
4. Submit the report.

Alternatively, email support@urbanhomeschool.co.ke with the subject line "Bug Report" and include the details above.

### Why am I getting a "Session Expired" message?

For security reasons, your session expires after a period of inactivity (default: 30 minutes). When this happens:

1. You will be redirected to the login page.
2. Log in again with your credentials.
3. Your work is not lost -- the platform saves your progress automatically.

If you are frequently logged out, ensure that your browser is not blocking cookies or clearing session data.
