# Student Guide

Welcome to your complete guide to using Urban Home School as a student. This guide covers everything from navigating your dashboard to chatting with The Bird AI, taking quizzes, and earning certificates.

---

## Dashboard Overview

When you log in, you arrive at your Student Dashboard. This is your home base for everything you do on the platform.

### Today's Tasks

At the top of your dashboard, you will see a list of tasks for today. These include:

- **Lessons** that are next in your course progress.
- **Quizzes** that are due or available to take.
- **Assignments** with upcoming deadlines.
- **Live sessions** scheduled for the day.

Click on any task to jump straight to it.

### AI Insights

The Bird AI analyzes your learning patterns and displays helpful insights on your dashboard, such as:

- Subjects where you are excelling.
- Topics that might need extra practice.
- Suggested study times based on when you learn best.
- Streak reminders to keep your learning momentum going.

### Progress Snapshot

This section gives you a quick overview of:

- **Courses in progress**: How far along you are in each course.
- **Completed courses**: Courses you have finished.
- **Current streak**: How many days in a row you have been learning.
- **Overall completion rate**: Your average progress across all courses.

## Using The Bird AI Tutor

The Bird AI is your personal tutor, available anytime you need help. Here is how to get the most out of it.

### Starting a Conversation

1. Click the **Bird AI** icon in the sidebar or the chat button on your dashboard.
2. Type your question or topic in the message box at the bottom of the screen.
3. Press **Enter** or click the send button.
4. The Bird will respond with an explanation tailored to your grade level.

### What You Can Ask

The Bird can help with a wide range of learning needs:

- **Explain concepts**: "What is the water cycle?" or "Explain how fractions work."
- **Solve problems**: "Help me solve 2x + 5 = 15" or "What is 3/4 times 2/3?"
- **Review topics**: "Quiz me on the parts of a plant."
- **Prepare for exams**: "Help me study for my Grade 6 science exam."
- **Get examples**: "Give me three examples of renewable energy."
- **Translate explanations**: "Explain this in simpler words" or "Can you explain in Kiswahili?"

### Getting Better Responses

- Be specific about what you need help with.
- Mention your grade level if The Bird has not already adapted.
- Ask follow-up questions if you need more detail.
- Try "Explain it like I am in Grade 4" if a response is too advanced.
- Say "Give me a step-by-step solution" for math and science problems.

### Conversation History

All your conversations with The Bird are saved automatically. To review past chats:

1. Open The Bird AI chat.
2. Click the **History** tab or scroll up to see previous messages.
3. Search for a specific topic using the search bar at the top.

## Browsing and Enrolling in Courses

### Finding Courses

1. Click **Courses** in the sidebar to open the course catalog.
2. Browse by category (Math, Science, Languages, Social Studies, Creative Arts, and more).
3. Use the grade filter to show courses for your level (Grade 1 through Grade 12).
4. Search for a specific subject or topic using the search bar.

### Course Details

Click on any course to see:

- **Course title and description**: What the course covers.
- **Syllabus**: A breakdown of all lessons and topics.
- **Instructor**: Who created and teaches the course.
- **Reviews**: What other students have said.
- **Duration**: Estimated time to complete.
- **Price**: Free or paid (with the amount shown in KES).

### Enrolling

- **Free courses**: Click **Enroll for Free** and you will be added immediately.
- **Paid courses**: Click **Enroll**, choose your payment method (M-Pesa, PayPal, or card), and complete the payment. You will gain access as soon as payment is confirmed.

## Completing Lessons and Tracking Progress

### Taking a Lesson

1. Go to **My Courses** in the sidebar.
2. Click on the course you want to continue.
3. You will see a list of lessons. Click the next incomplete lesson to start.
4. Read through the lesson content. Some lessons include videos, images, or interactive elements.
5. At the end of each lesson, click **Mark as Complete** to record your progress.

### Tracking Your Progress

- Each course shows a **progress bar** indicating how much you have completed.
- Your dashboard displays an overall progress summary.
- The Bird AI may remind you to continue courses you have not visited in a while.

## Taking Quizzes and Submitting Assignments

### Quizzes

1. Quizzes appear in your **Today's Tasks** or inside a course after completing relevant lessons.
2. Click on a quiz to begin.
3. Read each question carefully and select your answer.
4. Some quizzes are timed -- a countdown timer will appear at the top.
5. Click **Submit** when you are done.
6. Your score and correct answers are shown immediately.

### Assignments

1. Open the assignment from your tasks or from within a course.
2. Read the instructions carefully.
3. Type your response in the text box or upload a file (documents, images, or PDFs).
4. Click **Submit Assignment** before the deadline.
5. Your instructor will review and grade your work. You will receive a notification when feedback is available.

### Projects

Projects are larger assignments that may take several days or weeks:

1. Read the project brief carefully.
2. Work on your project over time, saving your progress as you go.
3. Submit your final work before the deadline.
4. Projects are often graded on multiple criteria, so review the rubric provided.

## Viewing Achievements and Badges

As you learn, you earn achievements and badges that celebrate your progress.

### How to Earn Badges

- **Course completion**: Finish a course to earn a completion badge.
- **Streaks**: Maintain a daily learning streak.
- **Quiz performance**: Score highly on quizzes.
- **Milestones**: Complete a certain number of lessons, courses, or study hours.

### Viewing Your Badges

1. Go to **My Profile** or **Achievements** in the sidebar.
2. You will see all the badges you have earned, along with the date you earned them.
3. Badges you have not yet earned appear greyed out with hints on how to unlock them.

## Using the Forum and Community Features

The forum is a place to connect with other students, ask questions, and share ideas.

### Browsing the Forum

1. Click **Forum** in the sidebar.
2. Browse posts by category: Latest, Popular, or Unanswered.
3. Click on a post to read it and see replies.

### Creating a Post

1. Click **New Post** in the forum.
2. Choose a category and add a descriptive title.
3. Write your post. You can format text, add links, and include images.
4. Add relevant tags so others can find your post.
5. Click **Publish**.

### Replying and Interacting

- Click **Reply** on any post to join the discussion.
- Click the heart icon to like a post you find helpful.
- If someone answers your question, mark it as **Solved** to help others find the answer.

### Forum Guidelines

- Be respectful and kind to other students.
- Stay on topic and use the right category.
- Do not share personal information.
- Report posts that break the rules.

## Managing Your Wallet and Payments

### Your Wallet

Your wallet tracks your balance and all transactions on the platform.

1. Go to **Wallet** in the sidebar.
2. View your current balance, recent transactions, and payment history.

### Topping Up Your Wallet

1. Click **Top Up** in the wallet section.
2. Choose your payment method: M-Pesa, PayPal, or Card.
3. Enter the amount you want to add.
4. Complete the payment.
5. Your wallet balance will update once the payment is confirmed.

### Paying for Courses

When enrolling in a paid course, you can pay directly from your wallet balance or use a payment method at checkout.

## Customizing Your AI Tutor's Personality

You can personalize how The Bird AI interacts with you.

### Accessing Settings

1. Open The Bird AI chat.
2. Click the **Settings** or gear icon.
3. You will see options to customize your experience.

### What You Can Customize

- **Response style**: Choose between detailed explanations, concise answers, or a mix.
- **Difficulty level**: Set The Bird to explain things at your grade level or challenge you with harder content.
- **Language**: Choose your preferred language for responses.
- **Personality**: Select a friendly, encouraging, or direct tone.

Changes take effect immediately in your next conversation.

## Voice Mode and Accessibility Features

### Voice Mode

Voice mode lets The Bird read its responses aloud.

1. In The Bird AI chat, click the **speaker** icon on any response to hear it read aloud.
2. To enable automatic voice responses, go to **Settings > Voice Mode** and turn it on.
3. Adjust the voice speed and volume to your preference.

### Accessibility

Urban Home School is designed to be usable by everyone:

- **Text size**: Adjust text size in your browser or device settings.
- **High contrast**: Use your device's accessibility features for better visibility.
- **Screen reader support**: The platform works with popular screen readers.
- **Keyboard navigation**: All features are accessible via keyboard.

## Getting Help from Teachers

If you need help that goes beyond what The Bird AI can provide:

1. Check if your course has an instructor available for live sessions.
2. Go to the course page and click **Contact Instructor** to send a message.
3. Join scheduled live sessions where you can ask questions in real time.
4. Post your question in the forum -- instructors and other students may be able to help.

## Account Settings and Privacy

### Managing Your Account

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to:

- Update your profile photo, name, and contact details.
- Change your password.
- Update notification preferences (email, SMS, or in-app).
- View your login history.

### Privacy

Your privacy matters to us:

- Your conversations with The Bird AI are private and only visible to you (and your parent if you are under 18).
- Your personal information is never shared with other students.
- You can request to download or delete your data at any time.
- For more details, review our Privacy Policy in the Settings page.

### Logging Out

Always log out when using a shared device:

1. Click your profile icon in the top right corner.
2. Click **Log Out**.
