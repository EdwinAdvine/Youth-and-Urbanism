# Store Guide

The Urban Home School Store is where you can browse and purchase educational products, learning materials, and merchandise. This guide walks you through browsing, purchasing, and tracking orders.

---

## Browsing Products

### Accessing the Store

1. Click **Store** in the sidebar.
2. The store homepage displays featured products and categories.

### Finding Products

- **Browse by category**: Click on a category to see all products in that group.
- **Search**: Use the search bar at the top of the store to find products by name or keyword.
- **Sort**: Sort results by popularity, price (low to high or high to low), or newest arrivals.

### Viewing a Product

Click on any product to see its detail page, which includes:

- **Product name and description**: What the product is and what it includes.
- **Images**: Photos or previews of the product.
- **Price**: Displayed in KES.
- **Availability**: Whether the product is in stock.
- **Reviews**: Ratings and feedback from other buyers.

## Adding Items to Cart

### How to Add Items

1. Browse to the product you want.
2. Click **Add to Cart**.
3. If the product has options (e.g., size or quantity), select them before adding to cart.
4. A confirmation will appear showing the item has been added.

### Viewing Your Cart

1. Click the **Cart** icon in the top navigation bar.
2. Your cart shows all added items with their names, quantities, and prices.
3. You can:
   - **Change the quantity** by clicking the plus or minus buttons.
   - **Remove an item** by clicking the remove or trash icon.
   - **Continue shopping** by clicking back to the store.

### Cart Total

The cart displays:

- Subtotal (sum of all item prices).
- Delivery fee (if applicable).
- Total amount to pay.

---

## Checkout Process

### Step 1: Review Your Cart

1. Click the **Cart** icon and review the items.
2. Make sure everything is correct (right products, quantities, and sizes).
3. Click **Proceed to Checkout**.

### Step 2: Delivery Information

If the order includes physical products:

1. Enter your delivery address (or select a saved address).
2. Choose your delivery option:
   - **Standard delivery**: Arrives within 5 to 10 business days.
   - **Express delivery**: Arrives within 2 to 3 business days (additional fee may apply).
   - **Pick-up**: Collect from a designated pick-up point (where available).

For digital products, this step is skipped -- you receive instant access after payment.

### Step 3: Payment

1. Choose your payment method:
   - **M-Pesa**: Enter your phone number, receive a prompt, enter your PIN.
   - **PayPal**: Complete payment via PayPal.
   - **Card**: Enter your card details.
   - **Wallet**: Pay from your Urban Home School wallet balance.
2. Click **Pay Now**.
3. Wait for payment confirmation.

### Step 4: Order Confirmation

After successful payment:

- You will see an order confirmation page with your order number.
- A confirmation email is sent with the order details.
- For digital products, download links are available immediately.
- For physical products, delivery tracking information will follow.

## Order Tracking

### Viewing Your Orders

1. Go to **Store > My Orders** (or **Settings > Order History**).
2. You will see a list of all your orders with:
   - Order number.
   - Date placed.
   - Items ordered.
   - Total amount.
   - Current status.

### Order Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Confirmed | Your order has been received and payment confirmed |
| Processing | Your order is being prepared |
| Shipped | Your order is on its way -- a tracking number may be provided |
| Delivered | Your order has been delivered |
| Completed | Order fulfilled (for digital products, this is immediate) |
| Cancelled | Your order was cancelled (refund will be processed) |

### Tracking a Shipment

If your order has been shipped:

1. Go to **My Orders** and click on the order.
2. Look for the **Tracking Number** or **Track Order** link.
3. Click to see the current delivery status and estimated arrival.

### Issues with an Order

If something is wrong with your order (wrong item, damaged, or not delivered):

1. Go to **My Orders** and click on the order.
2. Click **Report an Issue**.
3. Describe the problem and attach photos if applicable.
4. Our support team will respond within 2 business days.
